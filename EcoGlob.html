Recycling Programs

Find local programmes, schedules and how you can participate.

PJ Eco Recycling Plaza

Schedule: Tuesday - Sunday (9:00 AM - 4:30 PM)
Location: Jalan ss8/39, Sungai Way Free Trade Industrial Zone, 47300 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
Details: One stop learning center for recycling and drop-off station to archive zero waste in Petaling Jaya towards 2030

RE.UNIQLO

Schedule: Every day, also open during Holidays (10:00 AM - 10:00 PM)
Location: Certain Shopping Malls in Malaysia
Details: "RE.UNIQLO" is a framework that helps maximize the utility of the clothing we make, contributing to a circular society.

L'Occitane Malaysia - BIG Little Things

Schedule: Monday - Sunday (10am - 10pm)
Location: 1 Utama, Sunway Pyramid, and Suria KLCC, etc.
Details: Their goal is to mobilise more people to make big impact on the environment with the little things we do.
