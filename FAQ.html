FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Simple, actionable steps to reduce your energy consumption at home or work.

    Q1: What is EcoGlob?

    EcoGlob is a community-focused web application designed to promote sustainable living practices in your area. It helps users find local recycling programmes, access energy-conservation tips, join community gardening projects, and swap eco-friendly products with others.

    Q2: Who can join EcoGlob?

    Anyone in the community can join EcoGlob — whether you’re a homeowner, renter, student, or part of a workplace. Once you register, you can explore the features, join projects, list items for swap, and engage with others.

    Q3: Is there a cost to use EcoGlob?

    No. EcoGlob is free to use for all registered users. Our aim is to make sustainable living accessible and engaging for everyone in the community.

    Q4: How do I join a community gardening project?

    Simply log in, navigate to the “Community Gardening” section, browse the available projects in your area, and click “Join” for the one you’re interested in. You’ll be notified if your participation is confirmed.

    Q5: How does the product swap feature work?

    In the “Eco-Friendly Product Swap” section, you can list items you no longer need (provided they are reusable and eco-friendly), browse items listed by others, and connect to swap. You’ll handle the swap arrangement (meet-up or drop-off) directly with the other user.

    Q6: How do I find information about local recycling programmes?

    Go to the “Recycling Programs” section in our homepage, enter your postcode or locality, and you’ll see a list of programmes, collection schedules, locations, and guidelines on how to participate in each.

    Q7: Can I save favourite energy-conservation tips?

    Yes. Under “Energy Conservation Tips,” you can browse all tips and mark ones you’d like to save or refer back to later in your personal profile or dashboard.

    Q8: Is my data safe on EcoGlob?

    Absolutely. We take user privacy seriously. Your personal details are stored securely, and any public contributions (e.g., swap listings or gardening project posts) only display what you choose. Be sure to review our Privacy Policy for full details.
